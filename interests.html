Research Interests

Natural Language Processing

How and to what extent can computers acquire the ability to understand, interpret, and/or generate human language?

Computational Psycholinguistics

How can computational models/algorithms be used to better understand the ways in which human children acquire language?

Educational Applications

How can computational models of language be applied to improve language learning in children, particularly among underserved areas/demographics?
